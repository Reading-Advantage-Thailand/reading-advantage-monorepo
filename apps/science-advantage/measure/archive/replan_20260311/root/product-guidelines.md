# Science Advantage Product Guidelines

## Tone and Prose Style
- **Encouraging & Accessible:** The platform's voice is friendly, supportive, and clear. It is designed to motivate K-12 students and simplify complex scientific concepts without sacrificing accuracy.
- **Supportive for ELLs:** Language should be simple and direct to assist English Language Learners, avoiding unnecessary jargon or overly complex sentence structures.

## Visual Identity and UI Design
- **Clarity & Focus:** The user interface prioritizes learning objectives by minimizing distractions. Clear visual hierarchies and purposeful use of whitespace guide the student through the curriculum.
- **Accessibility:** Design for a diverse range of learners, ensuring high contrast, readable typography, and intuitive navigation across all devices.

## Brand Messaging and Core Values
- **Educational Equity:** We are committed to providing world-class science education to all students, regardless of their native language or geographic location. Our mission is to bridge the educational gap in East and Southeast Asia.
- **Empowerment through Data:** We value the role of the teacher and provide the tools necessary to make data-driven decisions that improve student outcomes.

## Language and Multilingual Support
- **Seamless L1 Scaffolding:** First-language (L1) support (translations, glossaries, explanations) is integrated as a toggleable layer. This provides immediate assistance without disrupting the immersive English-based learning environment.
- **Guardrailed AI Assistant:** A specialized, guardrailed chatbot assistant is available to provide context-aware help in both English and the student's first language, ensuring safe and focused academic support.
