## Notes for Content Authors

**Required Sections (in order):**
1. Introduction
2. Main Content
3. Key Vocabulary
4. Reading Passage
5. Summary

**Validation Checks:**
- Vocabulary entries must follow format: `- **Term** (Thai: translation) - Definition`
- Must include 8-12 vocabulary terms
- All vocabulary must have Thai translations
- Reading passage should be 300-500 words for Grade 3

**Tips:**
- Use clear, simple language appropriate for grade level
- Break content into digestible chunks with subheadings
- Include concrete examples students can relate to
- Make vocabulary definitions conversational, not dictionary-style
- Reading passages should tell a story or present interesting information
- Use active voice and engaging tone throughout
