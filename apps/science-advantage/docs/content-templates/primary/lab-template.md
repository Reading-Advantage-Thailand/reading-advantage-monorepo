## Notes for Content Authors

**Required Sections (in order):**
1. Introduction
2. Learning Objectives
3. Materials
4. Safety Notes (if applicable)
5. Procedure
6. Observations
7. Conclusion Questions
8. Summary

**Validation Checks:**
- Materials must follow format: `- [quantity] item name`
- Safety notes must use warning emoji: `⚠ text`
- Procedure must be numbered list starting with `1.`
- Procedure must have at least 3 steps

**Tips:**
- Write procedure steps in imperative mood (commands)
- Each step should be clear enough for students to follow independently
- Include timing estimates if certain steps require waiting
- Anticipate common mistakes and address in Teacher Tips
- Make observation prompts specific and guide students' attention
- Conclusion questions should promote higher-order thinking
- Consider safety at every step - when in doubt, add a safety note
- Test the procedure yourself or with a pilot group if possible
