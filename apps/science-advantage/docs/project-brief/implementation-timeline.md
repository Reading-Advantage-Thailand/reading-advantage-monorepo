# Implementation Timeline

## 6.1 Development Phases

**Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-6)**

- **Month 1-2:** Platform architecture and ecosystem integration
- **Month 3-4:** Core science curriculum development and virtual labs
- **Month 5-6:** Mobile applications and Reading Advantage integration
- **MVP Launch:** End of Month 6 to existing Reading Advantage schools

**Phase 2: Expansion (Months 7-12)**

- **Month 7-8:** Advanced analytics and teacher dashboard
- **Month 9-10:** Parent portal and cross-subject features
- **Month 11-12:** Offline access and live tutoring integration
- **Full Launch:** End of Month 12 to broader market

**Phase 3: Optimization (Months 13-18)**

- **Month 13-15:** AR/VR experiences and advanced AI features
- **Month 16-18:** International curriculum and corporate training
- **Market Leadership:** Established position in Thai K-12 market

## 6.2 Key Milestones

**Critical Milestones:**

- **Month 3:** Alpha version with core curriculum and basic integration
- **Month 6:** MVP launch to 100+ existing Advantage schools
- **Month 9:** 50% conversion rate of existing customers achieved
- **Month 12:** 100 new schools acquired with K-12 bundles
- **Month 18:** Market leadership position with 25% private school share

**Go/No-Go Decision Points:**

- **Month 3:** Technical feasibility and integration validation
- **Month 6:** Market validation and customer feedback
- **Month 9:** Business model validation and revenue projections
- **Month 12:** Scale readiness and resource allocation

## 6.3 Resource Allocation

**Development Team:**

- **Phase 1:** 8 developers, 2 curriculum specialists, 1 AI engineer
- **Phase 2:** 12 developers, 4 curriculum specialists, 2 AI engineers
- **Phase 3:** 15 developers, 6 curriculum specialists, 3 AI engineers

**Budget Allocation:**

- **Development:** 60% of total budget
- **Content Creation:** 20% of total budget
- **Marketing and Sales:** 15% of total budget
- **Operations and Support:** 5% of total budget
