# Resource Requirements

## 8.1 Human Resources

**Core Development Team:**

- **Project Manager:** 1 (experienced in EdTech ecosystem projects)
- **Technical Lead:** 1 (full-stack with AI/ML experience)
- **Frontend Developers:** 3 (React/Next.js, mobile experience)
- **Backend Developers:** 3 (Node.js, microservices, database)
- **AI/ML Engineers:** 2 (Python, educational data mining)
- **Mobile Developers:** 2 (React Native, iOS/Android)
- **QA Engineers:** 2 (automated testing, performance)
- **DevOps Engineer:** 1 (cloud infrastructure, CI/CD)

**Content and Education Team:**

- **Curriculum Specialists:** 2 (Thai science education experts)
- **Content Writers:** 3 (science education, bilingual capability)
- **Instructional Designers:** 2 (digital learning experience)
- **Subject Matter Experts:** 2 (Thai science curriculum consultants)
- **Reading Specialists:** 1 (cross-subject integration)

**Business and Operations:**

- **Product Manager:** 1 (EdTech experience, ecosystem thinking)
- **Marketing Manager:** 1 (Thai education market knowledge)
- **Sales Team:** 3 (existing Advantage relationships, school sales)
- **Customer Support:** 2 (bilingual, technical support)
- **Success Managers:** 2 (school implementation, teacher training)

## 8.2 Technology Infrastructure

**Development Environment:**

- **Development Tools:** GitHub, Jira, Slack, Figma
- **Testing Framework:** Jest, Cypress, automated testing pipeline
- **CI/CD Pipeline:** GitHub Actions, automated deployment
- **Monitoring:** Application performance monitoring, error tracking

**Production Infrastructure:**

- **Cloud Provider:** AWS (consistent with Advantage ecosystem)
- **Database:** PostgreSQL with RDS, Redis for caching
- **CDN:** CloudFront for content delivery
- **Storage:** S3 for user data and content assets
- **AI/ML Infrastructure:** SageMaker for model training and deployment

**Third-Party Services:**

- **Authentication:** Leverage existing Advantage SSO system
- **Payment Gateway:** Omise (Thai market leader)
- **Analytics:** Custom dashboard with existing Advantage data
- **Communication:** Line integration for Thai market

## 8.3 Budget Requirements

**Development Costs (18 months):**

- **Personnel:** ฿45M (average ฿25K/month per team member)
- **Technology Infrastructure:** ฿8M (cloud services, tools, licenses)
- **Content Creation:** ฿12M (curriculum development, multimedia)
- **Quality Assurance:** ฿5M (testing, validation, compliance)

**Marketing and Sales (18 months):**

- **Digital Marketing:** ฿6M (social media, search, content marketing)
- **School Sales:** ฿8M (sales team, travel, partnerships)
- **Events and Workshops:** ฿4M (teacher training, conferences)
- **Brand Development:** ฿2M (materials, presence)

**Operations and Support (18 months):**

- **Customer Support:** ฿6M (team, tools, 24/7 coverage)
- **Professional Development:** ฿3M (teacher training, certification)
- **Legal and Compliance:** ฿2M (privacy, education regulations)
- **Contingency:** ฿9M (15% of total budget)

**Total Budget:** ฿110M for 18-month development and launch period
