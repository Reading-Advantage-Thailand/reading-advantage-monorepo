# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

This competitive analysis provides strategic insights for Science Advantage's market entry into Thailand's K-12 science education sector. The analysis examines current market dynamics, key competitors, pricing strategies, and opportunities for differentiation.

## 1.2 Scope

- **Geographic Focus:** Thailand, with emphasis on major urban centers (Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket)
- **Grade Levels:** Primary (Prathom 1-6) and Secondary (Matthayom 1-6)
- **Subject Focus:** Science curriculum (general science, physics, chemistry, biology)
- **Competitor Types:** Established EdTech companies, traditional publishers, and emerging startups

## 1.3 Methodology

Research conducted through:

- Market analysis of Thai EdTech landscape
- Competitor product and pricing analysis
- Educational policy review
- Parent and teacher behavior patterns in digital adoption
