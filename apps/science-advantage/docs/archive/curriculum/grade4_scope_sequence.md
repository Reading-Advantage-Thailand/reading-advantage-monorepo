# Grade 4 Science Scope and Sequence

## Science Advantage App - Thai National Standards Alignment

**Total Instructional Time**: 80 hours (120 periods × 40 minutes)
**Periods per Week**: 3
**Total Weeks**: 40 weeks

---

## INSTRUCTIONAL MODEL

### Lesson Types

1. **Explicit Instruction (EI)**: Reading passage + embedded assessment
2. **Lab Investigation (LAB)**: Hands-on experiment + digital lab report
3. **Fun Review (FR)**: Engaging review activity to recharge
4. **Summative Assessment (SA)**: Comprehensive assessment

### Two-Week Cycle Pattern

- **Week A**: EI + EI + EI (3 reading lessons)
- **Week B**: EI + EI + LAB (2 reading lessons + 1 lab)
- **Every 4 weeks**: Replace one EI with FR, followed by SA

### Assessment Framework

**Formative (embedded after each lesson):**

- 6-10 multiple-choice questions (auto-graded)
- 3-4 short-answer questions (AI-scored, teacher review)
- Real-time progress tracking with adaptive difficulty adjustment

**Summative (every 4-5 weeks):**

- 25-30 multiple-choice questions
- 6-8 short-answer questions
- Lab report review component
- AI generates class report on misconceptions and mastery gaps

**Lab Reports (digital template):**

- Question/Problem
- Hypothesis (more developed than Grade 3)
- Materials
- Procedure
- Data Collection (tables, measurements)
- Analysis (patterns, relationships)
- Conclusion (claim supported by evidence)
- AI flags: missing units, incomplete data, unsupported conclusions, calculation errors

## DIFFERENTIATION ACROSS TRACKS

### High Track

**Reading:**

- Advanced vocabulary and concepts
- Extended explanations with real-world applications
- Preview of Grade 5 concepts

**Assessments:**

- Additional higher-order thinking questions
- More complex scenarios
- Analysis and evaluation tasks

**Labs:**

- Extended investigations with additional variables
- Independent procedure development
- Leadership and mentoring roles

### Medium Track (Standard)

**Reading:**

- Grade-level vocabulary
- Clear explanations with examples
- Appropriate depth and pacing

**Assessments:**

- Standard question difficulty
- Mix of recall, comprehension, application
- Scaffolded short answers

**Labs:**

- Guided procedures with structure
- Standard data collection
- Supported analysis

### Low Track

**Reading:**

- Simplified vocabulary with glossary
- More visual supports and diagrams
- Chunked text with frequent checks

**Assessments:**

- More scaffolded questions
- Visual supports and word banks
- Sentence frames for short answers
- Extended time options

**Labs:**

- Highly structured procedures
- Simplified data collection forms
- Step-by-step guidance
- More teacher support
