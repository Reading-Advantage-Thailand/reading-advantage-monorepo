# Grade 5 Science Scope and Sequence

## Science Advantage App - Thai National Standards Alignment

**Total Instructional Time**: 80 hours (120 periods × 40 minutes)
**Periods per Week**: 3
**Total Weeks**: 40 weeks

---

## INSTRUCTIONAL MODEL

### Lesson Types

1. **Explicit Instruction (EI)**: Reading passage + embedded assessment
2. **Lab Investigation (LAB)**: Hands-on experiment + digital lab report
3. **Fun Review (FR)**: Engaging review activity to recharge
4. **Summative Assessment (SA)**: Comprehensive assessment

### Two-Week Cycle Pattern

- **Week A**: EI + EI + EI (3 reading lessons)
- **Week B**: EI + EI + LAB (2 reading lessons + 1 lab)
- **Every 4 weeks**: Replace one EI with FR, followed by SA

### Assessment Framework

**Formative (embedded after each lesson):**

- 8-12 multiple-choice questions (auto-graded)
- 4-5 short-answer questions requiring explanations (AI-scored, teacher review)
- Real-time adaptive difficulty adjustment based on performance

**Summative (every 4-5 weeks):**

- 30-35 multiple-choice questions
- 8-10 short-answer questions
- Lab report analysis component
- AI generates detailed class reports on misconceptions and skill gaps

**Lab Reports (digital template):**

- Question/Problem with background
- Hypothesis with reasoning
- Materials list
- Detailed procedure
- Data collection (tables with proper units, calculations)
- Analysis (identifying patterns, calculating averages/rates)
- Conclusion (claim-evidence-reasoning format)
- AI flags: missing calculations, unsupported claims, incomplete reasoning, unit errors

**Period 1 (EI): The Respiratory System**

- Assessment: MC + short answer tracing blood flow

## ASSESSMENT SUMMARY

### Total Assessments Per Year

- **Formative**: ~96 embedded assessments (after EI lessons)
- **Lab Reports**: 20 digital lab reports with comprehensive AI feedback
- **Summative**: 10 unit assessments plus 3-part final exam
- **Projects**: 1 major integrated project with presentation
- **Portfolio**: Year-end portfolio with reflections

### AI-Generated Reporting Schedule

**After Each Summative Assessment (11 times/year):**

- Class-wide misconception patterns by strand
- Individual student mastery levels with growth trajectories
- Adaptive track placement recommendations
- Priority topics for reteaching or enrichment
- Comparison to grade-level benchmarks

**After Each Lab Report (20 times/year):**

- Scientific process skill development tracking
- Common experimental design errors
- Data collection and analysis quality metrics
- Variable control understanding
- Students requiring additional support
- Exemplary work highlights

**After Major Project:**

- Integration of multiple strands assessment
- Independent investigation skills evaluation
- Long-term project management abilities
- Cross-curricular connections demonstrated

**Year-End Comprehensive Report:**

- Growth trajectories across all 8 strands
- Mastery levels for Grade 5 standards
- Grade 6 readiness assessment
- Individualized recommendations for summer learning
- Adaptive track recommendations for Grade 6
