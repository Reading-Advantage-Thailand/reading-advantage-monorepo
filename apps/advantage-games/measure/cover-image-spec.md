# Cover Image Specification

All game covers for the Vocab Arcade must adhere to the following standards to ensure a cohesive and professional appearance.

## General Requirements
- **Dimensions:** 1024x1024 pixels (Square).
- **Format:** PNG.
- **Content:** A stylized digital illustration that represents the game's core concept and gameplay elements (not a direct screenshot).

## Composition & Layout
- **Central Focus:** The main character or primary gameplay mechanic should be the central focal point, but it should not overwhelm the scene.
- **Game Elements:** Secondary elements (enemies, collectibles, environment) should have significant weight and visibility to accurately convey the game's theme.
- **Text Placement:**
    - **Title:** The game's name must be prominently displayed at the top.
    - **Tagline:** A descriptive tagline should be placed either immediately under the title or at the bottom of the image.

## Visual Style & Theme
- **Art Style:** High-quality digital illustration, vibrant and engaging.
- **Theme:** RPG / MMORPG aesthetic.
- **Typography:** Fonts must reflect the RPG/MMORPG fantasy style (e.g., serif, gothic, or heroic styles depending on the specific game's sub-theme).
- **No Logos:** Do not include "Vocab Arcade" or developer logos on the cover image itself.
- **Color Palette:** No strict constraints, but it should be bright, colorful, and representative of the game's visual identity.
