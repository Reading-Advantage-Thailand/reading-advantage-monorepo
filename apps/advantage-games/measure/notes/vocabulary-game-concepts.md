# Vocabulary Practice Game Concepts

**Core Mechanic:** A target word (or translation prompt) is displayed. The player must identify, collect, or interact with the correct matching word among distractors. Score is tracked and converted to 1-10 XP at game end.

**Required Features:**
- Target word/translation display
- Correct answer + distractor words in game world
- Clear feedback on correct/incorrect interaction
- Difficulty and/or opponent choice screens
- RPG or casual theme

---

## Classic Arcade Adaptations

### 1. Puzzle Bobble (Bust-a-Move style)

**Theme:** Dragon hatchling clearing corrupted crystal caverns

**Mechanic:**
- A chain of word-orbs moves along a curved ceiling path
- Target translation displayed at bottom
- Aim and shoot word-bubbles upward into the chain
- Hit the orb matching the correct translation — both pop (clear)
- Hitting wrong word adds the bubble to the chain (risk of overflow)
- Clear all orbs or survive until timer ends
- Chain combos when popping causes adjacent matches

**Difficulty Choice:** Chain speed
- Crystal Cavern (slow)
- Deep Mine (medium)
- Core Chamber (fast, chain reverses)

**Opponent Choice:** None — race against the chain reaching the danger line

**Controls:** Aim + tap to shoot

---

### 2. Bubble Bobble

**Theme:** Twin dragons trapping word-spirits in bubbles

**Mechanic:**
- Single-screen platformer with platforms
- Enemies roam carrying word labels
- Target translation displayed at top
- Blow bubble at enemy — if word matches translation, bubble pops for bonus
- If wrong word, bubble floats away (enemy freed, respawns)
- Pop correct bubbles to collect points, clear the screen
- Wrong bubbles that reach the ceiling turn into hazard clouds

**Difficulty Choice:** Screen complexity
- Sunny Garden (few platforms, slow enemies)
- Dark Cavern (many platforms, medium)
- Void Nest (moving platforms, fast enemies)

**Opponent Choice:** Enemy type
- Slimes (slow, predictable)
- Goblins (jump between platforms)
- Wraiths (phase through platforms)

**Controls:** DPad + bubble button

---

### 3. Whack-a-Mole

**Theme:** Blacksmith hammering mischievous word-imp

**Mechanic:**
- Grid of holes (3×3 or 4×3)
- Target translation displayed prominently
- Word-imps pop up from random holes showing words
- Tap the imp with the correct translation
- Correct whack = imp stunned, points earned
- Wrong whack = imp laughs, hammer stuck briefly (cooldown)
- Imps that stay up too long without being whacked = life lost
- Speed increases over time

**Difficulty Choice:** Grid size + speed
- Apprentice Forge (3×3, slow)
- Master Smithy (3×3, fast)
- Grand Forge (4×3, very fast, multiple imps)

**Opponent Choice:** Imp behavior
- Shy Imps (one at a time)
- Sneaky Imps (fake-outs, pop up and hide)
- Swarm Imps (2-3 at once)

**Controls:** Tap/click holes

---

### 4. Doodle Jump

**Theme:** Spirit ascending the Tower of Words

**Mechanic:**
- Endless upward-scrolling tower
- Platforms labeled with words appear as you jump
- Target translation shown at top
- Jump only onto platforms with the correct translation
- Wrong platform = it crumbles, you fall (lose a life)
- Spring platforms give extra height, breakable platforms crack
- Screen wraps left-to-right
- Score = height reached

**Difficulty Choice:** Tower type
- Spirit Staircase (many safe platforms)
- Wizard's Ascent (balanced)
- Void Spire (few safe platforms, many crumbling)

**Opponent Choice:** None — survival against height

**Controls:** Tilt or tap left/right side

---

### 5. Tetris

**Theme:** Alchemist stacking word-blocks in the Great Furnace

**Mechanic:**
- Word-blocks fall from top, each block carries a word
- Target translation displayed at top of the well
- Blocks come in pairs: one correct translation + distractors
- Rotate and place blocks — when a correct word lands, the row glows and clears
- Wrong word placement = row solidifies (permanent, takes up space)
- Game over when stack reaches the top
- Score = rows cleared

**Difficulty Choice:** Fall speed
- Slow Burn (gentle speed)
- Hot Forge (medium)
- Inferno (fast, blocks accelerate)

**Opponent Choice:** Block complexity
- Simple Blocks (single words)
- Compound Blocks (multi-word phrases as distractors)
- Arcane Blocks (similar-looking words, harder to distinguish)

**Controls:** Rotate, move left/right, drop

## Viral Casual Adaptations

### 6. Castle Siege (Angry Birds style)

**Theme:** Trebuchet bombardment of enemy word-fortresses

**Mechanic:**
- Enemy fortifications on the right side of screen, each wall/tower labeled with a word
- Target translation displayed at bottom
- Drag and release trebuchet to launch a projectile
- Hit the structure carrying the correct translation — it crumbles, points earned
- Wrong hit = structure reinforces (takes extra hit), wasted launch
- Limited projectiles per round — accuracy matters
- Bonus for clearing all structures in one volley

**Difficulty Choice:** Fortress complexity
- Wooden Palisade (3 structures, simple shapes)
- Stone Keep (5 structures, stacked)
- Iron Citadel (7 structures, reinforced walls)

**Opponent Choice:** Garrison type
- Goblin Scouts (weak structures, no retaliation)
- Orc Guards (medium structures, archers shoot back)
- Dark Knights (strong structures, catapults fire back)

**Controls:** Drag to aim, release to launch (slingshot mechanic)

---

### 7. Blade Dancer (Fruit Ninja style)

**Theme:** RPG hero deflecting cursed word-blades in the arena

**Mechanic:**
- Cursed blades fly upward from the arena floor, each inscribed with a word
- Target translation displayed at top
- Swipe your sword to slash the blade carrying the correct translation
- Correct slash = blade shatters into light shards, combo meter rises
- Wrong slash = blade grazes you (lose a life), combo resets
- Bomb runes occasionally appear — slicing them = heavy damage
- Multi-slice combos when several correct blades are cut in one swipe
- Arena crowd cheers as combo streak grows

**Difficulty Choice:** Blade frequency
- Training Yard (slow, 1-2 blades at a time)
- Gladiator Pit (medium, 2-3 blades)
- Champion's Gauntlet (fast, 3-4 blades, more bomb runes)

**Opponent Choice:** Arena champion
- Apprentice Blade-Dancer (predictable blade arcs)
- Arena Veteran (varied trajectories)
- Grand Champion (spiral blade patterns, more bombs)

**Controls:** Swipe/drag to slash

---

### 8. Flappy Bird

**Theme:** Familiar navigating through the Word Gates

**Mechanic:**
- Continuous forward scrolling
- Gate pairs appear — left gate shows one word, right gate shows another
- Target translation displayed at top
- Tap to flap and fly through the gate with the correct translation
- Wrong gate = crash, lose a life
- Gate gaps vary in height
- Score = gates passed

**Difficulty Choice:** Gate frequency
- Gentle Breeze (wide gaps, slow)
- Mountain Wind (medium gaps, medium)
- Storm Front (narrow gaps, fast)

**Opponent Choice:** None — pure survival

**Controls:** Tap to flap

---

### 9. Zuma

**Theme:** Temple guardian defending the Word Serpent's path

**Mechanic:**
- A chain of word-orbs spirals toward a skull temple at the center
- Target translation shown near the temple
- Guardian shoots word-orbs from the center outward into the chain
- Shoot the correct translation orb — it matches and both disappear
- Wrong shot = orb inserts into chain, pushing it closer to the temple
- Chain combos: clearing orbs causes the chain to snap together, creating new match opportunities
- Game over if chain reaches the temple

**Difficulty Choice:** Chain speed
- Desert Path (slow)
- Jungle Trail (medium)
- Volcanic Rift (fast, chain accelerates over time)

**Opponent Choice:** Chain composition
- Simple Chain (unique words, easy to spot)
- Trick Chain (repeated words, similar spellings)
- Cursed Chain (bomb orbs that explode nearby words)

**Controls:** Aim + tap to shoot

---

### 10. Crossy Road / Frogger

**Theme:** Adventurer crossing the Realm of Words

**Mechanic:**
- Grid-based lanes: roads with carriages, rivers with logs
- Target translation displayed at top
- Hop forward one tile at a time (tap) or sideways (swipe)
- Safe tiles (lily pads, grass) carry words — only hop onto the correct translation
- Wrong tile = falls in river or hit by carriage (lose a life)
- Reach the far shore to complete a round
- Each round gets a new target word

**Difficulty Choice:** Realm width
- Village Crossing (6 lanes)
- Forest Path (8 lanes)
- Capital Gauntlet (10 lanes, faster traffic)

**Opponent Choice:** Hazard type
- Carriages only (predictable lanes)
- River + Carriages (mixed lanes)
- Chaos Realm (moving hazards, diagonal traffic)

**Controls:** Tap/swipe in direction to hop

## Sentence Game Candidates

The following concepts could also work well as **sentence games** with adaptation:

### Burger Time

**Theme:** Tavern cook assembling word-burger orders for hungry adventurers

**Mechanic:**
- Multi-level tavern kitchen with platforms and ladders
- Target sentence displayed on the order board at top
- Ingredients (words) are on upper shelves/platforms
- Walk under the correct next word to knock it down into your bun
- Wrong word drops = customer rejects order (life lost)
- Enemies (greedy rats, rival cooks) roam the kitchen — touch = lose a life
- Drop all words in correct sentence order to complete the order
- Complete multiple orders to earn bonus XP

**Difficulty Choice:** Kitchen size
- Small Tavern (2 levels, 4-word sentences)
- Busy Inn (3 levels, 6-word sentences)
- Grand Banquet Hall (4 levels, 8-word sentences, more enemies)

**Opponent Choice:** Kitchen pests
- Greedy Rats (wander randomly, slow)
- Rival Cooks (chase player, medium)
- Kitchen Goblins (patrol ladders, fast)

**Controls:** DPad movement, climb ladders

---

### Zuma (as Sentence Game)

**Adaptation:** The chain contains individual words of a sentence in random order. The guardian shoots the next correct word in sentence order into the chain. When a word is matched, it snaps into position. Complete the sentence chain before it reaches the temple.

### Tetris (as Sentence Game)

**Adaptation:** Falling blocks carry individual words. The target sentence is displayed at top. Players must arrange words into rows that form partial sentence fragments. A complete sentence row clears. Wrong word placement creates dead rows.

---

## Recommended Implementation Order

1. **Whack-a-Mole** — Simplest mechanic, high engagement, great for quick vocabulary drills
2. **Castle Siege** — Angry Birds recognition, satisfying physics destruction
3. **Puzzle Bobble** — Satisfying chain-reaction mechanic, visually appealing
4. **Blade Dancer** — Swipe mechanic, highly satisfying feedback loop
5. **Crossy Road / Frogger** — Familiar lane-crossing, clear vocabulary integration

## Technical Notes

All vocabulary games should:
- Use `VocabularyItem[]` with `{ term, translation }` pairs
- Display target word/translation prominently during gameplay
- Track: correct answers, total attempts, time
- XP formula: `Math.floor(correctAnswers * accuracy)`
- Support both touch and keyboard input
- Minimum 44×44px touch targets, 16px minimum text

### Word Display

- Each word should be visually distinct (bubble, brick, orb, etc.)
- Wrong selection feedback: shake, flash red, sound
- Correct selection feedback: sparkle, chime, particle burst
