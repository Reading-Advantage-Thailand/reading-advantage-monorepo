# Game Specification: Squire's Gauntlet

**ID:** `squires-gauntlet`  
**Theme:** Medieval Battlefield / Crossing  
**Mechanic:** Crossing Hazards (Frogger style)  

## Gameplay

1. **Objective:** Reach the other side of the battlefield by collecting words from a sentence in the correct order.
2. **Movement:** 
   - Control the Squire using DPad or arrow keys.
   - Discrete, grid-based or smooth movement across "lanes" of hazards.
3. **Lanes:**
   - **Hazard Lanes:** Charging Knights, Rolling Logs, Fireballs (hurt player, life loss).
   - **Support Lanes:** Moving platforms (logs, shields) that can carry the player across.
   - **Safe Zones:** Lanes with no hazards.
4. **Collection Mechanics:**
   - Word orbs are scattered on safe spots or floating on platforms.
   - Collect the word orbs in the correct order to complete the sentence.
   - If the word is **correct**:
     - Word is added to the "message" bar.
     - Play success chime.
     - Gain Score/XP.
   - If the word is **incorrect**:
     - Word is rejected (shake, red flash).
     - Squire is knocked back or loses a life.
     - Score penalty.
5. **Death Conditions:**
   - Collision with a hazard (Knights, Fireballs).
   - Falling into water (if applicable) or missing a platform.
   - Reaching zero lives.
6. **Sentence Completion:**
   - Complete the sentence and reach the far end of the gauntlet to pass the level.
   - Translation shown at the top.

## Educational Integration

- Focus on sequential word collection and sentence structure.
- High-pressure timing requires quick reading and comprehension.

## Visuals

- **Background:** Grassy battlefield with dirt roads, water channels, and mud.
- **Squire:** Small armored character with a banner.
- **Hazards:** Charging horsemen, rolling siege logs, flying fireballs.
- **Platforms:** Moving shields, wooden logs in water lanes.
- **Effects:** Dust clouds, splashes, magical glows for correct words.

## Controls

- **DPad / Arrow Keys:** Move the Squire (Up, Down, Left, Right).
- Mobile-friendly (touch optimized).
