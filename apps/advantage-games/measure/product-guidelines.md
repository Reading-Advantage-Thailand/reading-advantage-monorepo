# Product Guidelines

## Visual Aesthetic: Modern Clean & Bright Cartoon
*   **Target Audience:** Primary school (Ages 7-11) and Secondary school students (Ages 11-18).
*   **Style Profiles:**
    *   **Modern Clean (Secondary):** Engaging and professional, utilizing Material Design principles with soft shadows and clear hierarchy.
    *   **Bright Cartoon (Primary):** Friendly, vibrant character-driven designs using warm, inviting color palettes and large, approachable UI elements (e.g., Enchanted Library).
*   **Design Principles:** Utilize Material Design principles with rounded corners, soft shadows, and a clear hierarchy.
*   **Color Palette:** Use vibrant, high-contrast colors for game elements to ensure visibility against professional, clean backgrounds.
*   **Typography:** Prioritize legibility with modern, sans-serif fonts suitable for quick reading during gameplay.

## Interaction and Feedback
*   **Multi-Sensory Feedback:** Provide clear indicators for both correct and incorrect actions using:
    *   **Visuals:** Green/red color flashes, icon transformations, and celebratory animations.
    *   **Haptics:** Implement subtle screen shakes or vibrations (where supported) for incorrect answers to provide physical feedback.
    *   **Audio:** Use distinct, high-quality "success" and "failure" sound effects to reinforce performance.
*   **Performance Optimization:** Ensure all animations and effects are optimized to prevent performance degradation on lower-end devices.

## Gameplay Mechanics
*   **Adaptive Difficulty:** The games must dynamically adjust speed or complexity based on real-time player accuracy and response time to maintain an optimal "flow" state.
*   **Vocabulary Scope:** Every game session is strictly bound to a pre-supplied list of vocabulary terms and their translations.
*   **Device Responsiveness:** Interfaces must be fully responsive, supporting both desktop (keyboard/mouse) and mobile (touch) interactions seamlessly.
